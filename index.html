FILTHLE

How to play

Guess the FILTHY WORD in 6 tries. Every answer is a crude 5-letter word — you've been warned. 🍆

  • Each guess must be a real 5-letter word. Hit Enter to submit.
  • After each guess, the tiles change color to show how close you were.

What the colors mean

BOOBS

B is in the word and in the right spot.

FARTS

A is in the word but in the wrong spot.

NAKED

K is not in the word at all.

Modes

  • Daily — one word a day, same for everyone. New word at midnight Eastern (ET).
  • Endless — random words, replay forever. Press any key after a round to go again.

Toggle modes with the button up top. Works with your keyboard or the on-screen one.
